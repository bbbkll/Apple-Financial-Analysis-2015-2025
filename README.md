# Project Overview
This project analyzes the financial performance of Apple Inc. over the period 2015 to 2025.
It uses real annual financial data from Apple's official annual reports to calculate revenue, net income, and profit margins, and visualizes the results with charts.

# Data Source
- Apple Inc. Annual Reports (2015–2025)
- Unit: Billions of US Dollars

# Tools Used
- Python(using Jupyter Notebook)
- Pandas for data processing and analysis
- Matplotlib for data visualization

# Contents
1. Data table showing yearly Total Revenue and Net Income
2. Line chart of revenue and net income trends
3. Bar chart of net profit margin
4. Calculation of profit margin percentage

# Key Insights
- Apple's total revenue shows overall growth from 2015 to 2025.
- Net income increased significantly after 2020.
- Profit margin remains stable and strong, mostly between 20% and 28%.
- The company maintains high operational efficiency and consistent profitability.
